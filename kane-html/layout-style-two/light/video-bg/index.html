Mission

Just a snippet showing what we're all about

Roommates

We're going to be releasing some pretty amazing features that will help facilitate and manage all of the things going on in your living space. I would tell you more, but for now it's top secret and we hope you we enjoy the current features.

Landlords

We haven't forgotten about you. As long as your tenants show some interest, we'll be releasing Futon apps custom tailored for you to manage your tenants. Collecting rent on time - that's what we're all about.

Renting

No longer will you need to rent a flat by dealing with the Craigslist's, Zillow's, Trulia's of the web. Renters can find available or upcoming places to rent via their social graph and landlord's can have more data to help them decide who is best in their space.
